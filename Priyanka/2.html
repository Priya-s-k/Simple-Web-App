    Recruting Process

    Firstly I want to thank to Great Learning Platform for suggesting me to apply for this company.

    Tricon Infotech has 4 Rounds in their Recruting Process

  • First Round- Online Test

    • It's Online test.It consist of aptitude,logical,verbal and etc questions and 5 coding questions with easy,medium and hard level.On clearing this test u will get mail for Technical Interview-1

  • Second Round-Technical Interview-1

    • It's Type of Video Call.They will ask questions related to java,sql,python and some puzzels and questions based on your resume and live coding etc. On clearing this u will get mail Technical Interview-2

  • Third Round-Technical Interview-2

    • It's also Type of Video Call.They will ask questions related to java,sql,python and some puzzels and mainly questions based on Project and interships. On clearing this u will get mail for HR round

  • Last Round-HR Interview

    • It's also Type of Video Call.In this some HR related questions will be asked like related to relocate,strong and weak points etc. and Some clarification related to salary and bond , working Environment etc.
